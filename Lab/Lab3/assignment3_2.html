DCS - 3.2. Runge's phenomenon

This program interpolates samples from the function f(x) = 1 / (1+25x2), for different values of n. The points to be interpolated will be samples from the graph of the function f(x) = 1 / (1+25x2) taken uniformly from the interval [-1, 1], that is: xi = 2i/n - 1.
